Kitchener-Waterloo

The KW region (and Cambridge) is highly known for it's innovation and engineering talent. However, side by side with that engineering talent is a thriving design community centered around the UX practice. With companies like Shopify, Open Text, Square, Google and more in town, the design community has been growing steadily.

Catch events put on by DesignChats, Brew Over Design, Shopify Sessions and UX Waterloo.
