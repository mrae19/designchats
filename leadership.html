Matt
Rae

DesignChats Founder
Kitchener-Waterloo Lead

Matt founded DesignChats in 2015 to bring together designers locally in Kitchener-Waterloo. He noticed many of these designers were solo, or part of small teams. In an effort to grow the design field, DesignChats was born as a support network to these fellow designers. It all started with breakfast.

Evan
Fletcher

London Chapter Lead
Designer @RaceRoster

Evan was inspired by the activity happening in KW, and saw a need for a similar community in London. In 2016 he brought the idea of DesignerBreakfast meetups to Edgar and Joe's and the London DesignChats community was born. Evan now leads the London chapter.
